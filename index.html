About Us

Who are we?
Southern Truck Lines is a small VTC with Headquarters in Phoenix Arizona. We run classic and cab over trucks. We largely focus on Agriculture and Flatbed but have many other opportunities. We are a small community and have our own custom mods and paint jobs. We are very selective on who we hire.

VTC

What's a VTC?
A virtual trucking company is a group of people who all play Euro Truck Simulator and/or American Truck Simulator together. A virtual trucking company simulates the way a real trucking company would operate. Some of these VTCs run modded trucks and only play in singelplayer, while others host convoys and drive together on TruckersMP, a multplayer version of both ETS2 and ATS.
